About Vaishnavi Travels

Founded in 2012, Vaishnavi Travels & Self Drive Cars has been a trusted name in the travel industry, specializing in hassle-free self-drive rentals. Our mission is to empower travelers with the freedom to explore at their own pace, with a fleet of well-maintained vehicles and 24/7 support.

We started with a small fleet and have grown to serve thousands of customers across India. Our commitment to safety, cleanliness, and customer satisfaction sets us apart. Whether it's a family road trip or a solo adventure, we're here to make your journey memorable.

Our Values

  • Reliability: On-time delivery and pick-up.
  • Affordability: Competitive tariffs with no hidden fees.
  • Safety: All cars insured and serviced regularly.
